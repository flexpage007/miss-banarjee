A Letter From the Heart

For You, My Everything

My Cheerful Lady,

Today the world is a little more beautiful than it was yesterday — because eighteen years ago, you came into it. And somewhere along the way, you came into mine. I don't think I'll ever fully understand how lucky I am, but I spend every day feeling it.

You are the kind of person people write songs about — the warmth in the corner of your eyes when you laugh, the way you care so deeply for everything and everyone around you, the quiet strength you carry even when things get hard. You make the ordinary feel extraordinary just by being present in it.

Loving you has taught me what it means to truly see someone. Not just the parts they show the world, but the small things — the way you think, the things that make you nervous, the dreams you hold close. I see all of you, and I am in awe of every single part.

On this day, as you step into eighteen, I want you to know: I am proud of the person you are. The miles we might be apart, the days that are tough, the moments that feel uncertain — none of that changes what I feel for you. You are home to me.

I dream of all the roads we'll walk together — early mornings, late nights, adventures in cities we haven't been to yet, quiet evenings that feel like everything. I want to be the one beside you through all of it. And I intend to be.

So happy birthday, my love. This year, and every one after it, I hope you feel as cherished as you deserve to be. Because to me, you are the whole world.

Forever yours — Arko 💙
